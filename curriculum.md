  <summary><strong>Bitcoin</strong></summary>

##### Description

This department focuses on all aspects of Bitcoin: the global network, nodes, miners, software, hardware, and energy requirements.

##### Objective

Upon completion, students will be able to: 
- describe and explain Bitcoin to general audiences;
- understand the fundamentals of the Bitcoin Protocol, how to programmatically interact with the network via their node, command line, and custom software;
- develop custom solutions and contribute to the Bitcoin ecosytem
